Barcelona is one of Spain's fastest-growing padel cities. The Mediterranean climate allows outdoor play nearly year-round, and the court supply keeps expanding. These are the best options for playing padel in Barcelona.

## Best Padel Courts in Barcelona

### [FairPlay Padel Barcelona](/torneos/0615ca8b-9da7-4616-9133-90676d467f4f)

**Carrer del Foc 2, 08038 Barcelona (Sants-Montjuïc)**

9 outdoor padel courts (8 doubles + 1 single) next to Fira Gran Vía. Padel school, regular tournaments, and corporate events. Book via Playtomic or WhatsApp.

**Contact**: +34 693 525 939 · padel@fairplaypadel.com

### [CEM Olímpia - Padel](/torneos/5b021acf-7e6d-42b1-a255-0d7887f9b53e)

**Sant Martí, Barcelona**

5 padel courts and 4 tennis courts in the Sant Martí neighborhood. Hours: Mon-Fri 8am-11pm, Sat 8am-9pm, Sun & holidays 9am-9pm. Online booking available.

**Contact**: +34 932 662 395

## Playing Padel in Barcelona: What You Need to Know

- **Climate**: Barcelona's climate is ideal for outdoor padel. Open-air courts are a great choice from March to November. During the colder months (December-February), indoor courts are preferable.
- **Booking**: Playtomic is the most popular app in Barcelona for booking courts. You can also book directly with clubs via their website or phone.
- **Prices**: Prices in Barcelona range from 7€ to 18€ per person/hour. Morning slots tend to be more affordable.
- **Tournaments**: Barcelona has an active amateur tournament calendar throughout the year. FairPlay organizes regular tournaments open to players of all levels.
- **Transport**: Many courts in Barcelona are accessible by metro or bus. FairPlay is well connected via the L2 and L9 metro lines.
